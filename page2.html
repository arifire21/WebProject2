My Cat

I have a maine coon mix named Macy! She's about 9 years old.

Personality

Macy is a very vocal cat, always chirping whenever someone is near. She will not hesitate to claim a rug or random spot on the floor as hers, stretching out as long as she can.


Appearance

According to her adoption papers, she is partially a domestic mix, but she has the maine coon traits of:

  • M shape on her forehead
  • Floofy striped tail
  • Longhaired coat
  • Fluffy chest
  • Slight fluffy tips on her ears

She also has a white mark on her nose to match her chest and paws, which adds to her cuteness factor.
